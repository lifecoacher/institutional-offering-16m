Market Context

Why Life Settlements. Why Now.

Zero Market Correlation
Life settlement returns are driven by biometric events — entirely independent of equity, fixed income, or credit markets.
Regulatory Tailwinds
Over $200B in annual policy lapse value, growing institutional adoption, and evolving regulatory frameworks support continued market expansion.
A-Rated Carrier Guarantee
Equitable Financial Life Insurance (A-rated, A.M. Best) — the death benefit is a contractual obligation backed by one of America's largest insurers.
Asymmetric Downside
Maximum loss is capped at premiums paid. The death benefit is certain — only timing varies. The $0 cash surrender value means zero recovery friction.
900% Mortality Multiple
Life expectancy of 36 months vs. actuarial norm implies extreme impairment — providing high-conviction underwriting confidence for near-term maturity.
Capital Efficiency
At $23,156 per $1M of death benefit annually, this policy ranks in the top tier of cost-efficiency for institutional-grade life settlements.
